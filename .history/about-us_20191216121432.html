Meet Hibabox
(The Kiosk for the Mosque)

What we do

Double your pledges and donations

Fresh Idea

In the modern age, fewer of us carry cash. Till now, majority of the Mosques were restricted to only collect its donations by cash only as no other means were available. Soon, the days of shaking collection boxes and encouraging friends and family to give loose change will be a distant memories.

Unique Designs

Meet Hibabox, a multi-feature kiosk, builds for the mosque enabling them to accept donations quickly and simply via contactless, debit or credit card. Now you can grow your charity work and see your pledges and donations grow further and make a difference for the good causes and the community.

Who we are
