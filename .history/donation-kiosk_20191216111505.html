Hibabox Kisok

What is Hibabox

Hibabox Kiosk system was founded on the insight that a cashless society is having a negative impact on the charity and religious sectors. It helps remove the barriers to charity and good cause and enable a mosque to facilitate and create a more close relationship with Masjid members and donors. Hibabox helps mosques to dramatically increase donations by accepting cashless payments.

Reasons to choose Hibabox kiosk system:

• Accept Debit/credit card payments & donations.

• Security of funds collected. Funds straight into Bank account.

• Create fundraising campaigns through user account.

• Real time Donation Management.

• Effective Collection and distribution of Zakat, Fitrana, Sadqah etc..

• Event Ticket sales and program registration.

• Fundraising and pledge management.

• Automated Aazan and widgets.

Donor Management System

Mosques require a solution that eliminates the burden of managing multiple systems and integrates all aspects of donor management into one flexible, scalable, low-cost solution. It is so important to have the right donor management tool in place in order to avail full potential in raising funds and keeping a meaningful audit trail at the same time. With Hibabox, it is now possible to process all kind of donation payments i.e. contactless, debit card and credit card and recurring payments right through your computer, your website, Hibabox innovative self-Service Donation Kiosk* or by accessing your Hibabox portal on your computer remotely and anywhere in the world. All of this by using the latest in cloud technology and without the hefty price tag of an on premise solution. Other features include:

• Advance report analysis for planning and decision making.

• Security of funds collected. Funds straight into Bank account.

• Create fundraising campaigns through user account.

• Real time Donation Management.
