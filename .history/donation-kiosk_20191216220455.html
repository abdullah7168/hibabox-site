Hibabox Kisok Features

What is Hibabox

Hibabox Kiosk System was founded on the insight that a cashless society is having a negative impact on the charity and religious sectors. It helps remove the barriers to charity and good cause and enable a mosque to facilitate and create a more close relationship with Masjid members and donors. Hibabox helps mosques to dramatically increase donations by accepting cashless payments.

Reasons to choose Hibabox kiosk system:

  • Accept Debit/credit card payments & donations.
  • Security of funds collected. Funds straight into Bank account.
  • Create fundraising campaigns through user account.
  • Real time Donation Management.
  • Effective Collection and distribution of Zakat, Fitrana, Sadqah etc..
  • Event Ticket sales and program registration.
  • Fundraising and pledge management.
  • Automated Aazan and widgets.
Donor Management System

Mosques require a solution that eliminates the burden of managing multiple systems and integrates all aspects of donor management into one flexible, scalable, low-cost solution. It is so important to have the right donor management tool in place in order to avail full potential in raising funds and keeping a meaningful audit trail at the same time. With Hibabox, it is now possible to process all kind of donation payments i.e. contactless, debit card and credit card and recurring payments right through your computer, your website, Hibabox innovative self-Service Donation Kiosk* or by accessing your Hibabox portal on your computer remotely and anywhere in the world. All of this by using the latest in cloud technology and without the hefty price tag of an on premise solution. Other features include:

  • Advance report analysis for planning and decision making.
  • Real time data capture of the Donors.
  • Consolidation of duplication of donors and customer information to create a meaningful summary.
  • Generate year-end Tax summary for donors – customise cover letter to send along.
Membership Management

With Hibabox member database, your member data is secured in the cloud so multiple users can access it from a desktop, phone or tablet at the same time.

  • Gain trust from your community by engaging directly with the donors and members.
  • Free/fee-based membership registration.
  • Customize membership levels and automate renewals.
  • Member can sign up for membership through either Hibabox kiosk or masjid website and get access to their member portal.
  • Not just members can update their own info through member’s login area but can also renew their membership, buy event tickets, participate in online forum, print donation receipts and year-end tax summaries direct from their online account.
  • Your data is maintained safe, secure and automatically backed up periodically so you don’t have to worry.
Event Registration & Fee Management

Increase event attendance of a programme with online program registrations.
Hibabox event Registration module enables Masjid Admin s to manage programs and create new event for the Mosque (Both free and paid).
Once event campaign is created such as Quran Classes, Hajj and Umrah Seminars or weekly circles, you community can register programs online, through Kiosk or through Mobile app.

  • Registration process can be automated resulting in timely fee collection of any event.
  • Registration process can be automated resulting in timely fee collection of any event.
  • Manage multiple events efficiently.
  • Less hectic than to run around and chase payments from the attendees.
  • Increase event fee collections.
  • Donors can simply register for a programs by paying via Hibabox Kiosks either a one-time payment or can setup recurring fees..
Announcement Email and SMS
  • Simple and easy way to engage members, donors, push feeds for any event announcement and newsletters.
